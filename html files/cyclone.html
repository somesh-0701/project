Cyclone Preparations & Precautions

Be prepared and stay safe during cyclone emergencies

Preparations

  • Stock up on emergency supplies: food, water, and first aid kits.
  • Keep batteries, torches, and portable chargers ready.
  • Secure important documents in waterproof containers.
  • Trim trees and remove loose debris around your house.
  • Prepare an evacuation plan with your family.
  • Identify nearby cyclone shelters and safe zones.
  • Charge mobile phones and keep emergency contacts handy.
  • Listen to official warnings through radio, TV, or apps.

Precautions

  • Stay indoors and away from windows during the cyclone.
  • Turn off electrical mains and gas supply if required.
  • Avoid standing water to prevent electrocution risks.
  • Do not venture outside until the all-clear is issued.
  • Stay in the strongest part of the house, like bathrooms.
  • Keep pets and livestock in safe shelters.
  • Avoid using candles; use battery-operated torches instead.
  • After the cyclone, beware of fallen wires and damaged buildings.
