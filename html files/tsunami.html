Tsunami Preparedness & Precautions

Stay Safe. Be Prepared. Act Responsibly.

Preparations

  • Know the tsunami evacuation routes and maps in your area.
  • Prepare an emergency kit with food, water, first aid supplies, and medications.
  • Identify higher grounds and practice evacuation drills regularly.
  • Keep a battery-powered radio to receive emergency alerts.
  • Secure valuable documents in waterproof containers.
  • Ensure all family members know the emergency contact information.

Precautions

  • Move to higher ground immediately when you feel an earthquake.
  • Stay away from coastal areas, rivers, and estuaries during alerts.
  • Do not wait for official warnings if you notice water receding rapidly.
  • Never attempt to watch the tsunami waves; they travel faster than you can escape.
  • Help others evacuate, especially elderly and disabled individuals.
  • Stay informed through emergency broadcasts and avoid rumors.

For more information, visit the National Preparedness Site.

Stay safe and informed.
