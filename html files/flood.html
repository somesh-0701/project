Flood Preparation & Precautions

Be prepared and stay safe during floods by following these key steps.

Preparations

  • Prepare an emergency kit with food, water, and essentials.
  • Keep important documents in a waterproof bag.
  • Plan an evacuation route in case of flooding.
  • Charge mobile phones and keep power banks ready.
  • Move valuable items to higher ground.
  • Listen to weather forecasts and flood warnings.

Precautions

  • Avoid walking or driving through floodwaters.
  • Turn off gas, water, and electricity supplies if needed.
  • Stay on higher ground during heavy rainfall.
  • Do not drink floodwater; boil or purify water before use.
  • Watch out for loose power lines and debris.
  • Help others, but ensure your safety first.

For more information, visit the official flood safety guide.
