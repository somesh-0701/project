Landslide Safety Guide

Key Preparations and Precautions to Stay Safe During Landslides

Preparations

  • Identify landslide-prone areas in your locality.
  • Prepare an emergency kit with essential items.
  • Stay updated with weather forecasts and warnings.
  • Plan and practice evacuation routes in advance.
  • Strengthen slopes and prevent water logging around your property.

Precautions

  • Move to higher ground immediately if a landslide occurs.
  • Avoid construction on steep slopes or unstable terrain.
  • Stay away from rivers, valleys, and landslide-prone areas during heavy rain.
  • Do not cross flooded or debris-filled roads.
  • Listen to authorities and evacuate when instructed.
Stay alert, stay safe, and help protect your community from landslides.
